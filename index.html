DREAM BATTLE

DreamBattle is a fighting game where YOU get to create your own fighters using AI! Simply describe whatever fighter you can imagine and let the AI do the rest. Creating an Image of whatever fighter you described and narrating an exciting duel against another fighter!

Your fighter can be anything! A dragon, A superhero, A lightning tiger, An explosive meteor, Goku from dragonball, A black hole, Hercules the god of strength. anything! Your imagination is the limit!

Dreambattle has 2 gamemodes, Quick game and Chain battle

CHAIN BATTLE

In CHAIN BATTLE players create their fighters and control their fighters actions outplaying each other to become the last man standing. The fight begins with an opener but stops, then players enter their fighters actions.
